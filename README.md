# 🚀 InterviewIQ AI

An AI-powered interview preparation platform that helps candidates improve their interview performance through resume analysis, personalized interview questions, AI-generated feedback, voice-based mock interviews, and structured learning resources.

---

## 📌 Overview

InterviewIQ AI is designed to simulate real interview experiences and provide actionable feedback to help users prepare for technical and behavioral interviews.

The platform analyzes resumes, generates role-specific interview questions, evaluates responses, conducts voice-based interviews, and provides a comprehensive learning hub covering interview preparation topics.

---
